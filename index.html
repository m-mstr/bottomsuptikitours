Bottoms Up Tiki Tours

Bottom's Up is Chincoteague Island's most unique and intimate boat charter. Let your worries melt away as our tiki captain and crew whisk you away from bustling city life for an unforgettable adventure. Our 40' tiki charter boat can accommodate up to 40 people.

Bites and Bottles

You can bring your own drinks and snacks or let us take care of the food details with our catering options or specialty cruises.

Safe and Secure

At Bottom's Up, our guests' comfort, safety, and satisfaction are our top priority. All of our charters include a Coast Guard licensed captain, bottled water, and a bathroom on-board.

Tiki Vibes

Our boat features an amazing sound system and our DJ curated playlists will quickly have you jammin'. You can also hook into our sound system and DJ your own party.

Time With Your Tribe

Bottom's Up Tiki Tours are the perfect way to celebrate any occasion! Birthdays, proposals, wedding parties, girls/guys night out, graduation parties, team building, corporate meetings, retirement, bachelorette/bachelor, anniversary parties, or any reason you can think of.. or for no reason at all.

How Do You Tiki?

We have a multitude of different cruises and adventures to match your vibe. All cruises can be booked as a private charter if you prefer. Give us a call to create your personal specialty cruise.

Cruises

NO SHOES, NO SHIRT, NO PROBLEM!

11:00AM-1:00PM Lunch Channel Cruise

You can do lots of things on this mid day cruise.... soak up the sun while checking out the Island sights or have a catered lunch on board.

THE LICENSE TO CHILL CRUISE

2:00-4:00PM Sit Back and Relax

This tour gives you a "License to Chill" Spend your day exploring the waterways around Chincoteague's campgrounds. We can stop at a waterfront bar or search for dolphins. Casual cruising with no strings attached.

THE 5:00 SOMEWHERE TOUR

4:00-6:00PM Local Hotspot Stops

It may be close to happy hour, but there's no judgement here because there is always a five o'clock cruise somewhere. Escape to the islands on this two hour tour. Add on our snack or dinner catering options for a truly memorable day.

WHEN THE SUN GOES DOWN TOUR

6:00-9:00PM 3 Hour Sunset Cruise

Relax while we drift into the sunset with a drink in our hand and some great island vibes playing in the background. Add on a dinner package or some delicious snacks. Maybe we will see the elusive Green Flash!

BOTTOM'S UP BRUNCH ON THE BAY TOUR

10:00AM-1:00PM Morning Cruise

Saturday & Sunday Only

Irish coffee preferred... or Mimosas... or Bloody Mary's.. the choices are endless. Hey Early birds...Let's cruise on the peaceful morning waters around Chincoteague Island. Catered Brunch Buffet included. Time to unwind and relax in the morning sun.

THE SUMMER NIGHTS TOUR

Specialty Cruise - Call to Book!

What is way better than bar hopping??? Jumping on our floating tiki! This cruise is the perfect remedy for the late night party people... Cruise the waterways under the stars, crank up the tunes and have a starlit dance party. Disco ball included!

Special Events

FULL MOON TIKI CRUISE

8:00-11:00PM Only On Full Moons

Watch the full moon rise from the best seat in the house on this smooth ride around the island. If you've never been on the waters at night to see the beautiful stars and moon, you are in for a magical treat!

FORTH OF JULY FIREWORKS CRUISE

One night only - July 4th - The best way to see the Chincoteague Island Fireworks on July 4th is from a tiki boat. Float in the waterways while sipping a cocktail and listening to our fourth of July playlist. America at is best! No traffic, no crowds!

ANNUAL PONY SWIM VIP EXPERIENCE

July 26th (Times TBD)

Get VIP seats to one of the most unique attractions on the Eastern Shore. Call early to reserve your seats as we fill up quickly! This is a once in a lifetime experience. Get up close and personal as the ponies make their annual water crossing!

CHINCOTEAGUE CARNIVAL FIREWORKS CRUISE

July 29th - The best way to see the Chincoteague Carnival Fireworks on July 29th is from a tiki boat. Float in the best possible location while sipping a cocktail and watching the finale to the Chincoteague Volunteer Fireman's month long Carnival. America at is best! No traffic, no crowds!

CAMPGROUND SUNSET CRUISE AND CRABFEAST (Specialty Event)

This unique tour starts with a cruise around the island where will stop at one of Chincoteague's famous campgrounds for an authentic Eastern Shore crabfeast with all the fixings. After the feast we will board the boat to experience the absolute best sunset Chincoteague Island has to offer.
